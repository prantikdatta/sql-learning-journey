# Case #006: The Vanishing Diamond

## Case Summary

The priceless Heart of Atlantis necklace disappeared during a high-society gala at the Fontainebleau Hotel - Heart of Atlantis. Out of many guests questioned, only two provided actionable leads:
- **Clint Eastwood (Actor):** Overheard a suspect planning a rendezvous: *"Meet me at the marina, dock 3."*
- **Vivian Nair (Consultant):** Spotted a man wearing a **navy suit** and a **white tie**, holding an invitation code that ended with the letter **"-R"**.

## Investigation Logic

The investigation began by filtering the guest list and dress registry to match the specific physical and circumstantial profile described by Vivian Nair. 

By querying for:
1. Invitation codes ending in `R`
2. Attire descriptions containing both `navy suit` and `white tie`

The database narrowed the pool down to a prime suspect. This target was cross-referenced with the `marina_rentals` log for `dock_number = 3` to fulfill Clint Eastwood's tip, directly tying Guest ID 105 to the escape plan.

The suspect's final interrogation record was pulled, revealing the following admission:

> "I was the one who took the crystal. I guess I need a lawyer now?"

## Culprit

**Mike Manning**

## Verdict

Mike Manning is confirmed as the thief responsible for stealing the Heart of Atlantis necklace. The case was solved by cross-referencing gala invitation data, event attire registries, marina dock records, and the final signed confession.

✅ Solved

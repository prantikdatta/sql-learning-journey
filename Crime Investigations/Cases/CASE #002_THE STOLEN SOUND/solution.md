# Case #002: The Stolen Sound

## Case Summary

A rare vinyl record worth over $10,000 was stolen from West Hollywood Records on July 15, 1983.

Witnesses described the thief as someone wearing:

- Red bandana
- Gold watch

## Investigation Logic

The witness clues were used to filter suspects based on appearance and accessories.

The matching suspects were:

- Tony Ramirez
- Mickey Rivera
- Rico Delgado

Their interviews were then reviewed.

Rico Delgado stated:

> "I couldn't help it. I snapped and took the record."

## Culprit

**Rico Delgado**

## Verdict

Rico Delgado is confirmed as the thief because he matched the witness description and confessed to taking the record.

## Case Status

✅ Solved

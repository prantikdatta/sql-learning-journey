# Case #004: The Midnight Masquerade Murder

## Case Summary

Leonard Pierce was found dead in the garden during a masked ball at a Coconut Grove mansion on October 31, 1987.

Witnesses mentioned:

- A hotel booking at The Grand Regency
- Room 707
- Suspicious phone activity

## Investigation Logic

Hotel records led to person ID 11, who was overheard asking:

> "Did you kill him?"

Phone records showed that person ID 11 contacted person ID 58.

A later phone record revealed another lead:

> "I will do it. Only if you give me that nice Lambo of yours."

The mention of a Lamborghini shifted the investigation toward vehicle ownership records.

A Lamborghini owner who was also a carpenter was identified as Marco Santos.

His final interview stated:

> "I ordered the hit. It was me. You caught me."

## Culprit

**Marco Santos**

## Verdict

Marco Santos is confirmed as the mastermind behind Leonard Pierce's murder. The case was solved by chaining hotel records, surveillance notes, phone records, vehicle ownership, occupation data, and the final confession.

## Case Status

✅ Solved

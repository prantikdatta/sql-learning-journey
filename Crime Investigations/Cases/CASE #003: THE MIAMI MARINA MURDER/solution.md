# Case #003: The Miami Marina Murder

## Case Summary

A body was found near the docks of Coral Bay Marina on August 14, 1986.

The crime scene report mentioned two people nearby:

- Someone living on Ocean Drive
- Someone whose name matched a partial pattern

## Investigation Logic

The Ocean Drive clue identified Carlos Mendez.

The name-pattern clue identified Raul Gutierrez.

Their interviews revealed that a nervous person had checked into a hotel on August 13, and the hotel name included "Sunset".

Hotel and surveillance records showed that person ID 8 left suddenly at 3 AM.

The confession record stated:

> "Alright! I did it. I was paid to make sure he never left the marina alive."

Person ID 8 was confirmed as Thomas Brown.

## Culprit

**Thomas Brown**

## Verdict

Thomas Brown is confirmed as the murderer because witness statements, hotel records, surveillance activity, and confession records all point to him.

## Case Status

✅ Solved
